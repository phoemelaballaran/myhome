There was a huge concrete water slide. I was at the top and in front of me was a musician. He seemed sad, but in a "the world is too beautiful and I can't handle it" kind of way. As he was about to go down the slide, I quickly put together a collage with a line from his song and three pieces of magazine cutouts. I wanted to let him know that I appreciated his art. I managed to finish the collage in time. He smiled as I handed it to him, then he went down the slide. I followed him, surprised to discover that the end of the slide was in nearshore waters instead of a swimming pool. The musician was standing not far from where I landed. He transformed into someone I'm in love with in real life.
